IQ by Joe Ide


Joe Ide Biography

Joe Ide is a man of Japanese-American descent. He was born and raised in South Central (LA), and spent most of his free time reading Sherlock Holmes novels. He pursued higher education and went to school for a Post-Gradutae degree before deciding to pursue a career as a screenwriter. When this failed, however, Ide decided to attempt to write a book, using his favorite childhood series as inspiration. His debut novel proved to be a great success, and he can now be found working on hisnext piece in Santa Monica, California.

Summary

IQ is a novel that follows the story of Isaiah Quintabe, a high-school dropout living in southern Los Angeles. IQ is not your average dropout, however, as he is actually one of the smartest people in the area. In fact, he works as a detective for hire, similar to Sherlock Holmes. A key difference from Sherlock Holmes is his clients. Rather than getting hired by the Queen of England, Isaiah takes all of the cases in the bad part of LA that the police are either afraid of or do not want to deal with. The book includes multiple mysteries, starting with a child abducter, then moving to an almost-murdered rapper, and more.

Main Characters

These are the most prominent characters in the novel, and the ones that readers should be familiar with.

  • Isaiah Quintabe, a.k.a. IQ

    Isaiah is a high school dropout that grew up in the bad part of LA. He is a smart kid that made a name for himself through his amazing intellect and cunning detective skills.

  • Boyd

    A poor, deranged man obsessed with young children, especially girls. Has failed multiple kidnap attempts, and will be the culprit in Isaiah's first case of the novel.

  • Deronda

    A girl who dreams of becoming a celebrity solely off of her physical features. Once modeled for a fast food billboard, then got no further. Sticks around Isaiah in hopes that he can help her out.

  • Marcus Quintabe

    Isaiah's brother and previous roomate. Although Isaiah frequently mentions him as a big part of his life, Marcus is never actually in the novel, as he died in a hit-and-run.

  • Calvin Wright, a.k.a. Black the Knife

    A famous rapper who hires IQ to find the man who attempted to have him killed. He is also miserable and lonely, and in a poor mental state, even though he lives a life of luxury.

  • Juanell Dodson

    A young man in a tough financial situation, becomes Isaiah's roomate after Marcus. Low level criminal and drug dealer, but later aids Isaiah in his mysteries after trying to lead him into a life of crime. (Dodson = Ghetto Watson)

Personal Review

Personally, I loved the story. My two favorite types of novels are mysteries and intelligent stories, and this was a perfect blend. The way Isaiah managed to analyze situations was simply jaw-dropping, even in the first 15 minutes of the story. The only reason why I would not give this a five-star rating is that, at times, he almost felt "too good". He tended to get guesses right on his first try and figure most things out without difficulty.

Final Rating: 4/5 Stars
